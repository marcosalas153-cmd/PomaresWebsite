SERVICES

FACIAL

It is a skincare treatment designed to cleanse, hydrate, and revitalize the face. It includes steps such as deep cleansing, exfoliation, extraction of impurities, massages, and the application of masks or serums tailored to the skin’s needs.

DERMAPEN/MICRONEEDLING

This treatment helps the skin restore its superficial layers, assisting with acne, acne scars, spots, and improving texture. What microneedles do is create a controlled micro-injury to the skin, which the body recognizes as something it needs to repair, strengthen, and improve in quality.

MORPHEUS8

Morpheus8 is an innovative aesthetic medical treatment that combines microneedling with fractional radiofrequency energy to rejuvenate and tighten the skin, stimulating collagen and elastin production. This procedure can improve fine lines, wrinkles, skin texture, acne scars, stretch marks, and body contouring, using radiofrequency to penetrate the deeper layers of the dermis and subcutaneous tissue.

DERMAPLANE

It is an aesthetic treatment in which dead skin cells and fine hair are gently removed from the skin’s surface. The result is smoother, brighter, and more even skin, which also allows creams to be absorbed better and makes makeup look more even.

MICROPEELINGS

They are gentle treatments that help renew the skin by eliminating dead cells on the surface. Light substances are applied to stimulate the skin’s natural regeneration, improving its texture, reducing small spots, and giving it a more radiant and even appearance.

THERMAGE

A cosmetic procedure that uses radiofrequency energy to heat the deeper layers of the skin, stimulating the natural production of collagen. This helps reduce sagging and wrinkles, tighten the skin, and improve its firmness and texture.
